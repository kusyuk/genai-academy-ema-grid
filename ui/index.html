Welcome to EMA Grid

Project Brief & Continuity

EMA Grid is an advanced, decision-intelligence expansion of our Cohort 1 prototype, EMA (Elderly Medical Assistant).

While EMA focus on assistant for elderly patients, EMA Grid focus on providing decision intelligence for healthcare professionals. Helping the hospital, medical officers and public health workers to make informed decisions based on real-time data gained from EMA. For this demo I include EMA and EMA Grid side by side to show the continuity and integration of the two.
